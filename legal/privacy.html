1. Who We Are & Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how Sentinel Resilience Partners LLC ("Company," "we," "us") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with the CookCapable website, web application, and any associated mobile applications (the "Service"). This Policy is incorporated into, and should be read together with, our Terms of Service.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as follows:

4.2 Reference Lookups

When you scan or search for a book or food item, the App sends only the search term (such as an ISBN or UPC code) — not your account information — to the Google Books API, Open Library, or Open Food Facts to retrieve catalog information. These are outbound, largely anonymous lookups, governed by each provider's own privacy practices.

4.3 Legal & Safety

We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, or legal process, or where we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of the Company, our users, or the public.

4.4 Business Transfers

If the Company is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to confidentiality obligations.

5. Camera & Device Permissions

The App requests camera access to scan barcodes (for cookbooks and pantry items) and to photograph recipe pages and book covers for on-device optical character recognition. Barcode/text scanning and recognition are performed on your device; we do not transmit your camera feed to our servers. If you save a recipe with a photo, that photo is stored as part of Your Content and synced to your account as described above. You may decline camera access and use manual entry instead.

6. Data Storage, Security & Retention

The Service is local-first: Your Content is stored on your device and, if you are signed in, privately synced to our database hosted by Supabase, protected by row-level security so that only you can access your own data. Data in transit is encrypted (HTTPS/TLS). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain your account and Your Content for as long as your account is active. You may delete individual items at any time within the App, use the "Clear all data" option in Settings to erase your synced data, or request full account deletion by emailing info@sentinelresiliencepartners.com. We will delete or anonymize your data within a reasonable time after a verified deletion request, except where we are required to retain certain records (such as billing records) for legal or accounting purposes.

7. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & Export: Use Settings → "Export backup" to download a full copy of your data at any time.
  • Correction: You can edit or delete any of Your Content directly within the App.
  • Deletion: Use "Clear all data" in Settings, or email us to request deletion of your account.
  • Subscription management: Cancel anytime via Settings → "Manage / Cancel Subscription."

8. California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to: know what personal information we collect, use, and disclose; request deletion of your personal information; correct inaccurate personal information; and not be discriminated against for exercising these rights. We do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising, so there is no "opt-out of sale" mechanism required. To exercise any of these rights, email info@sentinelresiliencepartners.com; we will verify your request using your account email before acting on it.

9. European/UK Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, our legal bases for processing your information are: performance of a contract (providing the Service and processing subscriptions), legitimate interests (securing and improving the Service), and consent (where applicable, such as camera access). You have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or port your data, and to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at info@sentinelresiliencepartners.com.

10. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and we require account holders to be at least 18 years old (see Terms of Service, Section 3). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

11. International Data Transfers

Our service providers (including Supabase and Stripe) may process and store data in the United States and other countries. By using the Service, you understand that your information may be transferred to and processed in countries that may have different data protection laws than your own.

12. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to keep you signed in and to cache app data for offline use. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or cross-site tracking technologies.
